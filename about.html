Event planning has been my passion for almost 20 years—what began with family and friends has grown into a business rooted in thoughtful design and personalized experiences. My focus is on intimate events where every detail is carefully considered, and each element is tailored to reflect the client’s unique personality and vision.

From venue selection to custom design, I take a hands-on approach to ensure a cohesive, elevated experience from concept to completion. I’m known for my meticulous attention to detail and for creating thoughtful concepts that resonate with both hosts and guests.

Whether it’s an intimate dinner, a milestone celebration, or a small wedding, each event is executed with elegance, precision, and an unwavering attention to detail.
