My Story

Event planning has been my passion for almost 20 years, rooted in thoughtful design and personalized experiences.My passion for event planning began when I was a little girl, growing up in Chicago, inspired by my mom, a consummate entertainer. She frequently hosted dinner parties and celebrations, creating an atmosphere of enjoyment. I loved the excitement so much that I’d sneak down at night just to listen in. As I grew older, I began to plan parties and events for my family and friends, including extravagant birthday parties for my own children. What started as a hobby gradually evolved into a fulfilling career.

What To Expect

I specialize in intimate gatherings—thoughtfully designed events for 2 to 50 guests—where every detail is intentional and each element reflects a distinct personality and vision. I work closely with hosts to bring their ideas to life, offering creative direction, vendor sourcing, and hands-on coordination. My goal is to alleviate the stress of planning so they can be fully present for the celebration.

From venue selection to custom design, I take a detail-oriented approach to ensure a cohesive and elevated experience from concept to execution. Whether it’s an intimate dinner, a milestone celebration, or a small wedding, each event is crafted with elegance, precision, and genuine care.
